Global Cultures Club

This is the achievement that I'm most proud of. I started the Global Cultures Club with two friends of mine at school, and became the president this year. We host club events twice a month to learn from each other about customs and traditions of different countries over the world. We've learned traditional Spanish dance, cooked and tasted Latin American food, hold a Thanksgiving reunion, taught Chinese calligraphy for the Lunar New Year, and hosted a school-wide World Language Festival.
